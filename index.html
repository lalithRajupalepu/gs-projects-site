About Us

GS PROJECTS PVT LTD is a specialized consulting firm focused on pharmaceutical factory setup and compliance. Based in India, we help local pharma manufacturers bring their facilities to life — from concept to commissioning.

We offer end-to-end solutions for setting up manufacturing units across a wide range of dosage forms including:

  • Oral solid and liquid formulations
  • Injectables
  • Skincare and cosmetic products

Our core expertise covers:

  • Facility layout and GMP-compliant design
  • Cleanroom & HVAC planning
  • Equipment selection and utility mapping
  • Regulatory documentation and audit readiness

Whether you're building a new plant or upgrading an existing one, we ensure your facility meets the highest quality standards and is ready for local regulatory approvals.
